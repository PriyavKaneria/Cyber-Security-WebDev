Security Starts From Your Home

Be aware and make others aware about these.

A Mobile recharge shop
is a place where scamsters can
gain access to your mobile no.
and misuse it.
Debit Card skimming
happens when the PIN is revealed
to another person even for
short while.
Keylogger is a
malicious program that may be
installed in the victim's computer
for stealing passwords.
Shopping online has to be
done keeping guidelines in mind
as one can become a victim of phishing
or fake websites.
Call Spoofing happens through
apps that enable a person with
criminal intent to change one's no.
and voice to impersonate other.




Ransomware is a type of malicious
software that threatens to publish
the victim's data or block
it unless ransom is paid.
Cyber Stalking is the use
of the internet or other electronic
means to stalk by misusing
imformation uploaded on social media.
Profile Hacking happens when
your email or social networking
site is accessed by a probable
stalker who then compromises it.
Children who are vulnerable to loneliness,
low self-esteem can fall prey
to dangerous online games that
may become addictive and harm them.
